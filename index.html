About me

Currently I am a third year graduate student of  School of Social and Behavioral Sciences in Nanjing University. I received my B.A. in Sociology from Nanjing University in 2019. In the same year, I  was admitted to study for a M.A. degree under the supervision of Prof. Yuxiao Wu. I'm expected to graduate in 2022 and I hope to pursue a PhD degree in the future.

 Research Intetests

  • Inequalities and Stratification
    My research focuses on the inequalities and its social outcome. Under the guidance of Prof. Wu, we have completed a paper about the relationship between extracurricular education and educational inequality, the paper is under peer reviewed now. My dissertation hopes to explore profound correlation between economic inequality and human mental health.
  • Work and Labor Markets
    Basically, I'm concerned about youth employment. Using survey data, I try to analyze the the employment dilemma of young people.
  • Computational Sociology
